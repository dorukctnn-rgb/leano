Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a measurement?

Tap the Measure tab, choose Quick (3 measurements), Standard (6), Full Body (13), or Custom, and enter your values.

Are progress photos really free?

Yes. You can take unlimited front, side, and back photos completely free. Photos are stored only on your device and never uploaded anywhere.

What is included in the free version?

  • 3 full tracking sessions
  • Unlimited progress photos
  • Interactive body map
  • BMI & health metric calculations
  • Badge system

What does Leano Pro include?

Leano Pro unlocks unlimited tracking sessions, AI weekly summaries with plateau detection, advanced analytics, and more. Available for $4.99/month or $29.99/year.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Leano → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Is my data private?

All your measurements and photos are stored locally on your device. We never collect, share, or have access to your personal data.

How is Body Fat % calculated?

Leano uses the U.S. Navy method, which estimates body fat percentage from your height, waist, hip, and neck circumference measurements.
