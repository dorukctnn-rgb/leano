Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 The short version

Leano does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All your measurements and photos stay on your device. We cannot access your information.

1. Information We Collect

Leano is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Body measurements you enter
  • Progress photos you take or import
  • App settings and preferences
  • Goals you set

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Photos

Progress photos taken or imported within Leano are stored exclusively on your device. We do not upload, access, process, or share your photos in any way. When you delete the app, all photos stored by Leano are removed.

3. AI Features

The AI Weekly Summary feature analyzes your measurement data to provide insights. Only numerical measurement data (not photos) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. No personally identifiable information is included. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.

4. Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

5. Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

6. Camera and Photo Library

Leano requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to take and save progress photos. This access is used only when you explicitly initiate a photo action within the app.

7. Notifications

If you enable weekly reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers to deliver these notifications.

8. Children's Privacy

Leano is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
